The way it should be.

Hello, I am Snippy, I am a friend who will accompany you in your NGS adventure and will help with finding useful information for your SNPs from raw VCF file.

Access Anywhere

You can access Snippy on any device any where, it can be accessed from PC to phone with the same functionality. Easy to read and access on any device.

You are Secured

Your results are secured! Snippy is designed in Django, which is providing Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF) protection - best security for the web tools.

One Destination

You don't have to leave Snippy while working with your SNPs. All steps and tools are available in the same place. No jumping between software or even browser tabs.

Trusted Sources

Snippy is using trusted tools for SNP analysis. Oncotator is designed by well trusted Broad Institute and String is supported by EMBL and SIB.
